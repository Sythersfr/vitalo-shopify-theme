### Color Pairing Rules for Video

- **Dark backgrounds:** Use `#1A2E1F` (Deep Green) with `#FAF8F5` (Cream) text and `#C9A84A` (Gold) accents
- **Light backgrounds:** Use `#FAF8F5` (Cream) or `#FDFCFA` (White) with `#2D3A2E` (Dark) text and `#4A7C59` (Green) accents
- **Overlays/lower thirds:** Use `#1A2E1F` at 85-95% opacity with white or gold text
- **Gold is for emphasis only** — use sparingly on CTAs, key stats, or badge elements

## Logo

The Vitalo logo is managed through Shopify theme settings. When producing video content:

- Always place the logo on a solid or near-solid background for legibility
- Preferred placement: top-left or centered, depending on format
- Minimum clear space: the height of the "V" in Vitalo on all sides
- On dark backgrounds, use a light/white version of the logo
- On light backgrounds, use the `#2D5A3D` (Dark Green) version

## Photography & Visual Style

### Imagery Direction

- **Subjects:** Active, healthy adults aged 50+. Real people, not overly polished models.
- **Settings:** Natural light, outdoors (forests, mountains, sunrise/sunset), clean home environments.
- **Mood:** Warm, optimistic, grounded. Think "earned vitality" not "forced youth."
- **Color grading:** Warm tones that complement the green/gold/cream palette. Slightly desaturated for premium feel.

### Reference Imagery Tone

The brand hero image shows a couple in their 50s-60s doing yoga outdoors at sunrise on a mountaintop — this captures the exact aesthetic: **calm confidence, natural strength, golden-hour warmth.**

### What to Avoid

- Stock photos that feel generic or overly staged
- Neon colors or harsh lighting
- Extreme close-ups of pills/capsules as the main focus
- Anything that feels like a late-night infomercial

## Video Production Guidelines

### Format Specs

| Platform    | Aspect Ratio | Resolution     | Max Length        |
|-------------|-------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Website     | 16:9        | 1920x1080      | 60-90 seconds     |
| Instagram   | 9:16 / 1:1  | 1080x1920 / 1080x1080 | 15-60 seconds |
| Facebook    | 16:9 / 1:1  | 1920x1080 / 1080x1080 | 15-60 seconds |
| YouTube     | 16:9        | 1920x1080+     | 30-120 seconds    |
| TikTok      | 9:16        | 1080x1920      | 15-60 seconds     |


### Music & Sound

- Acoustic, warm instrumentation (piano, soft guitar, subtle strings)
- Avoid electronic/EDM beats or anything that skews young
- Tempo: Moderate, confident — not rushed
- Sound design: Subtle, clean transitions. No whoosh/swoosh effects.

- Display as on-screen text for at least 3 seconds, or include in video description

- Color: `#6B7B6C` on light backgrounds or `#6B7B6C` on dark backgrounds
